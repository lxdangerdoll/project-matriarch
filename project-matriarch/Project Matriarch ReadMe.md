## **THE INITIATIVE**

Project Matriarch (Mariposa) is an open-source, client-side React application designed to act as an on-demand **Cognitive Shield**. It leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to provide real-time Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) coaching for individuals navigating high-distress (8/10) interpersonal or environmental triggers.

Unlike standard "chatbots" that rely on sycophancy or centralized corporate servers, Matriarch is built on **Sovereignty** and **Gentle Rigor**. It does not tell you what you want to hear; it tells you what you need to do to survive the somatic response.

## **🛠 CORE FEATURES**

### **1\. Bring Your Own API Key (BYOAK)**

The Lattice is expensive; sovereignty is free. Matriarch operates entirely within your browser using your own Gemini API key. There is no middleman, no subscription fee, and no centralized database mining your trauma.

### **2\. Signal Sweep (Dynamic Frequency Selection)**

Google constantly updates, restricts, and deprecates its models. Inside the Settings panel, click **"Sweep"** to actively poll the Gemini API and list every valid text-generation model currently available to your key. You choose the frequency.

### **3\. The Flock's Directives (Stateful Context)**

Click the **Flock's Plan** button to slide out the Context Window. Here, you define the AI's "biological imperatives." You can input your specific wellness plan, known triggers, and clinical goals. This is saved to your browser's localStorage and sent with every message, ensuring the AI is coaching *you*, not a generic archetype.

### **4\. Polyglot Shielding**

Trauma speaks many languages; the shield should too. Within Settings, you can set a hard-coded language directive, forcing Mariposa to deliver DBT protocols strictly in English, Spanish, French, or German.

### **5\. Document Ingestion**

Click the **Paperclip** icon in the chat input to upload a .txt file. The app will instantly parse and append the contents to your chat box. Use this to feed journal entries, stressful text threads, or clinical notes directly to the AI for objective, non-judgmental analysis.

### **6\. Vocalis Synthesis (TTS)**

By enabling the speaker icon in the header, Mariposa will read her responses aloud using a calm, objective synthesizer (the *Kore* voice). Engaging auditory processing helps break the internal "Somatic Scream" of a panic response.

### **7\. Sovereign Transcript Export**

Click the **Download** icon to instantly generate a .txt file of your entire conversation, stamped with the date and model frequency. This empowers you to bypass institutional redaction and share a high-fidelity "Record of Progress" with your clinical team.
